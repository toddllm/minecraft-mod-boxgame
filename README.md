# Trap Platform Mod - The Arena of Fear

**Trap Platform** is a Minecraft Fabric mod (1.21) that introduces a chaotic, wave-based arena survival minigame. Players are trapped on a small platform and must survive an onslaught of deadly traps, mobs, and environmental hazards controlled by an ominous entity known as **The Box**.

- **The Box**: A floating, glowing Crying Obsidian entity that watches over the arena and attacks players with **Rings of Evoker Fangs**.

- **The Mystery**: A burning **"F"** structure generates near spawn.

- **Wave 10**: A burning **"A"** appears.

Upon reaching Wave 15, the true boss is unleashed in the Overworld.
- **Name**: **FEAR**
- **Type**: An **Evoker** riding a **Ravager** ("The Beast of Fear").
- **Powers**:
    - **Eternal Storm**: Summons a permanent thunderstorm and midnight darkness.
    - **Fire & Lava**: Rains down Large Fireballs and creates Lava pools.
    - **Beast Master**: If dismounted, Fear will capture and ride any nearby passive mob (Horses, Pigs, etc.).
- **Lore**: You will receive a **Lore Book** with his backstory and exact coordinates when he spawns.

## Installation
1.  Install **Fabric Loader** for Minecraft 1.21.
2.  Drop the mod jar into your `mods` folder.
3.  Launch Minecraft and join the world.

## Commands
- `/enter`: Alternative way to enter the arena.
- `/give @s trapplatform:teleporter`: Give yourself the teleporter item.

## Development
- **Build**: `./gradlew build`
- **Run Client**: `./gradlew runClient`
